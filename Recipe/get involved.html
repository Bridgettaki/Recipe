Follow the Instructions

      1.Make the cinnamonroll dough and knead for 10 min
      2.Let the dough rise for 1 hour
      3.Roll out the dough, spread with the filling and roll up
      4.Slice the cinnamon rolls into 12 equal pieces
      5.Let the rolls rise for another hour
      6.Back for 30 min or until golden brown
